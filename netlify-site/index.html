Add by photo

Take one photo of a shelf or a few items. It proposes a list, nothing is saved until you confirm each line, quantities and expiry dates are its best guess, not a reading.

Add by barcode

Scan a packaged item's barcode (or type it in) and it looks the product up on Open Food Facts, a public product database. Name, size, ingredients, allergens and nutrition come from that database, not from AI guesses. You confirm before anything saves.
